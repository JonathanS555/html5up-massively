Housing Price
Prediction

In recent months my roommate and I have been looking to make our first house purchase in the city of Tampa, Florida. Amongst indecision and uncertainty about pricing and important factors to consider when purchasing a house, I developed a predictive model to estimate house prices using the Ames Housing dataset, which includes 79 features describing various aspects of residential properties. Using python, scikit-learn, pandas, matplotlib, and seaborn, I successfully implemented feature engineering, advanced regression techniques (random forest, gradient boosting), and model evaluation using Root-Mean-Squared-Error (RMSE) to achieve accurate predictions of house prices, demonstrating proficiency in data preprocessing, feature selection, and implementing machine learning algorithms.
